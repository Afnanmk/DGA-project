  1. Log in to the National Single Sign-on portal via the Balady platform.
  2. Select Construction License Services, then Service Construction.
  3. License Services, then the required municipality.
  4. Select the engineering office, then enter the number and year of issuance of the building service, and attach a copy of the deed.
  5. Submit the request to the selected engineering office, then complete the information and attachments in the engineering office and send it to the municipality.
  6. Verify the information of the request, attachments and approvals by the municipality.
  7. Approve the request and issue a SADAD payment. Pay the fees and print the license.
